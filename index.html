About us

Led by a small group of locals who are as passionate as they are knowledgeable about their city, we like to think of Prague as a fantastical kitchen that contains a myriad of flavours and many hidden wonders and surprises.

Meet to Eat Prague Foodie Tour takes you to an eclectic mix of the finest venues in the city, on an enchanting route through the historical centre that perfectly weaves together the most interesting culinary hot spots. From traditional beer halls, grand cafe’s, architecturally iconic restaurants and hidden local venues...it’s one surprise after another..you will even experience a private beer tasting in an Old Prague Palace. All consumption is included in the price, and whilst the emphasis is upon degustations, you are free to indulge to your own satisfaction.

Meet to Eat Prague Foodie Tour takes you behind the scenes of this 1000 year Old Bohemian gastronomic epicentre. From traditional Czech meals to modern innovative Czech dishes, savoury and sweet, hot or cold, as locals we open doors to a culinary nirvana that would otherwise remain closed. Our food and drink pairings and tastes en-route are many and varied, and by the end of the tour you will have satisfied your stomach, excited your taste buds and quenched your thirst. Bon Appetit...or as we say in Prague “dobrou chut”.
